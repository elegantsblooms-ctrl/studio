# **App Name**: ElegantsBlooms

## Core Features:

- Product Display & Selection: Showcase bouquets with images, names, prices, delivery times, and tags ('Bestseller', 'Trending'), enabling selection via price-filtered category cards. Includes elegant hover animations (scale + glow) for product cards.
- Add-Ons & Upsell UI: Present a section for optional add-ons like chocolates, teddy bears, and premium wrapping, allowing users to select them to enhance their order.
- Checkout Simulation UI: Provide a user interface for collecting delivery details (name, phone, address, message for card), displaying an order summary including add-ons, and a mock 'Proceed to Payment' button.
- Informational & Trust Sections: Display 'How It Works' steps, aesthetic customer review cards, social proof (e.g., '2000+ Happy Customers'), same-day delivery assurances, and an Instagram-style gallery preview.
- Urgency & Conversion UI: Feature an urgency section with messages like 'Limited Slots Available Today!' and a 'fake' countdown timer to encourage immediate purchase decisions.
- Policy & Legal Agreement UI: Clearly display policies such as 'No Cash on Delivery' and 'No Refunds after order confirmation', including a mandatory checkbox for agreement to terms & conditions before checkout.
- Direct Contact Floating Button: Implement a sticky floating WhatsApp button for quick customer contact and support on mobile devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme evoking softness and luxury. Primary elements use a delicate mauve-pink (#CE7E96), while the background features a subtly tinted white (#FBF9FA) for an ethereal feel.
- An elegant magenta-purple (#D926D9) serves as the accent color, adding a touch of vibrancy for calls to action and highlighting key elements.
- Gold accents are reserved for subtle premium touches, such as icons, important headings, or decorative elements, to reinforce the soft luxury brand identity.
- Headlines utilize 'Playfair Display' (serif) for an elegant and sophisticated presence, while body text uses 'Poppins' (sans-serif) to maintain a modern and readable feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use elegant, simple, and clean line-art or subtle gradient icons that complement the soft luxury and Gen-Z aesthetic, enhancing the rounded UI and glassmorphism elements.
- A mobile-first, fully responsive design emphasizing clean, card-based UI with soft shadows, rounded corners, and subtle glassmorphism effects for a modern, premium look.
- Smooth scrolling, subtle hover effects (e.g., scale and glow on product cards), graceful transitions between sections, and understated sparkle or glow effects throughout the interface for an immersive premium experience.
